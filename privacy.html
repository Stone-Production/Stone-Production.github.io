Privacy Policy

Welcome to Stone Music's Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Discord music bot.

Information We Collect:

  • To provide you with premium features, we collect your Discord user ID.
  • Your Discord guild ID is collected to deliver the bot's services to your server.

How We Use Your Information:

  • We utilize your Discord user ID to grant premium features, and your Discord guild ID to offer the bot's services.
  • Your personal information is not shared with third parties, except for payment processors like Patreon or PayPal, which are necessary for premium subscriptions.

Your Information's Security:

  • We employ reasonable measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
  • Patreon or PayPal store payment information, adhering to their privacy policies and security practices.

Changes to This Policy:

If substantial modifications are made to this policy, we will inform you through our website or the bot itself.

Contact Us:

If you have inquiries about this policy, please reach out to us at contact@stonemusic.in.
